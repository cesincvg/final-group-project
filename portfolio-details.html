About Us

Storm’s Angels was created in honor of the founder's first dog “Queen Night Storm”. She was rescued from a puppy mill. She ended up becoming Diabetic and then slowly lost her eyesight and hearing as she aged.

Storm lived 7 years as a Diabetic and was managed at home. She developed degenerative myelopathy around 11 years old and was in a wheelchair until she passed away from bladder cancer at almost 14.
