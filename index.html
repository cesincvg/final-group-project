Jessica

Jessica is a 3 year old tabby/calico mix. She is very friendly and lovable. She has been with us over a year because she is FIV positive. Cats with FIV can live long and healthy lives. FIV is not easily transmitted to other cats. If animals are fixed the only way to transmit it is through a deep bite wound so cats with FIV can live in a home with other friendly felines. Jessica has been waiting patiently for a home of her own.
